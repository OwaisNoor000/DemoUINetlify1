Companionship Within the Gaming World

We at Ludius believe in bringing about a community where everyone can interact with each other without the fear of judgement, and build each other up in games.

Bridging different gaming communities together

Every game is an experience, we want every gamer to have the chance to experience the diverse and unique worlds that every game offers, bringing together different communities to discuss and explore everything those worlds have to offer.

Gaming Skills Improvement

By bringing together gamers in a manner that is controllable by them, they will be able to curate the players that they play with, allowing them to improve their own skills in the way they want to.

  • Looking For Group Posts

    Take your first step by posting a LFG post to get your match-making partners! Players will be able to interact with posts and send invitations to the host to play with eaach other.

  • Profile Customization

    Your profile matters in attracting players across Ludius to play video games with you. Choose the games you play when setting up your profile to let others know what games you play !

  • Chat with Players

    Invitations/Messages can be sent to your friends through here. Start a conversation with them while having a fun and pleasant gaming experience together !
